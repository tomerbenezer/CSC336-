Balloon Rides Information

Tethered Balloon Rides

  • $20/adult & $10/kids (12 and under)
  • Balloon anchored to the ground with 50' lines
  • Rides last a few minutes before descending
  • No reservations - first come, first served
  • CASH only - pay at the balloon with correct change preferred!

Balloon Festival Safety & Helpful Information

Ballooning requires very light winds and no thermal activity to be safe. Most balloons fly in the early morning and late evening hours. Please stay after the airshow to enjoy the splendor of many balloons launching.

Rides are subject to weather conditions and the availability of pilots and airplanes.

Group rates for admission are not available during the Annual Hot Air Balloon Festival Weekend.
