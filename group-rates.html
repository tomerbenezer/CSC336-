Flying Circus Group Admission & Rates

* * * * Admission Prices * * * *

Admission tickets are only sold at our gate on the day of the Airshow. No advance purchase is available (except for Season Passes).

Regular Admission:

  • Adults: $20.00
  • Military Members: $15.00* (With valid Military ID: Act/Rsv/NG/CG/Ret)
  • Children 5-12: $10.00
  • Children Under 5: FREE

Scout Group Admission:

  • Adult Leaders: $15.00
  • Scouts age 13+: $15.00
  • Scouts age 5-12: $10.00

* See the Flying Circus Scout Page for additional information on scout group pricing.

** Scout Group Rates Are Not Available during the Annual Hot Air Balloon Festival Weekend. **

Group Rates

For groups of 20 or more adults (ages 13 and up), the rate is $15 per adult. The child admission (ages 5-12) is $10.00 and is not discounted. If your group qualifies for the discounted rate, please send an email to info@FlyingCircusAirShow.com, or call the Flying Circus at 540-439-8661, and a representative will contact you.

**Group Rates Are Not Available during the Annual Hot Air Balloon Festival Weekend.**

Aircraft Ride Prices

  • Open Cockpit Biplane (Waco/Travel Air) - Two Passengers
