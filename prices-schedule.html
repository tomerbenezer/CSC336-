2024 Flying Circus Airshow & Special Events Schedule

Every Sunday, May thru October

Gates open at 11:00 AM

Pre-Show starts at 2:00 PM

Airshow starts at 2:30 PM and ends at approximately 4:00 PM

Schedule is subject to change - check our website for the latest updates

Admission Prices

  • Adults: $20.00
  • Military Members: $15.00 (with valid ID)
  • Children 5-12: $10.00
  • Children Under 5: FREE

For group rate information, check our webpage: Flying Circus Group Rates

*Group Rates Are Not Available during the Annual Hot Air Balloon Festival Weekend*
